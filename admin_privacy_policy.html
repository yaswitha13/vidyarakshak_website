1. Introduction

Welcome to VidyaRakshak. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the App. The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our App for a variety of business purposes described below. We process your personal information for these purposes in reliance on our legitimate business interests, in order to enter into or perform a contract with you.

4. Data Protection

All student data is securely stored using industry-standard encryption and security protocols. Data access is strictly limited to authorized school personnel only.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share student data with any third parties. Data is used exclusively by authorized teachers and administrators for educational monitoring and student welfare.
