About VidyaRakshak

VidyaRakshak is an AI-Enabled Early Warning System fundamentally designed to eradicate student dropout rates across rural India. Born out of a deep necessity to secure the future of millions of children, our platform acts as an intelligent bridge between school administrators, dedicated teachers, and concerned parents.

By meticulously tracking holistic metrics such as daily attendance consistency, behavioral patterns in counseling, and academic risk factors, our system predicts and highlights students who are at high risk of discontinuing their education. This allows educators to intervene proactively rather than reactively.

The ecosystem delivers real-time notifications, a highly confidential counseling record tracker, and an easy-to-use smart dashboard that visually categorizes student risks into High, Medium, and Low tiers. We believe every child deserves an unbroken path to success.
